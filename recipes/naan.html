Garlic Naan

Description

This Garlic Naan bread is light, soft, fresh, and filled with flavor! It is so good that you will be making it again and again. It is made using all-purpose flour. Traditionally it is made in Tandoor. You can make it at comfort of your home on a stovetop or in an oven. These pillowy soft naan breads are so addictive, you will love how quick and easy this recipe is to make!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup warm water, or more as needed
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups bread flour, or more as needed
  • ¼ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¼ cup chopped cilantro (Optional)
